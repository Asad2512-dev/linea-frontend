Who We Are

A small, focused team practicing responsive spatial planning.

Established in Portland, Oregon, Studio Linea arose from a desire to move away from standardized, modular development. We design spaces that respond directly to their geographic coordinates—utilizing salvaged Douglas fir, local structural concrete mixes, and raw steel elements that weather over time.

We work within a variety of contexts, from cabins in the Cascades to renovations governed by strict historic guidelines in Portland's older neighborhoods. We partner directly with local builders and woodworkers, ensuring that details are sketched, tested, and resolved collaboratively on-site.

Our Values

  • Material Honesty: We avoid synthetic finishes; wood, concrete, and metal should look like what they are.
  • Context First: We analyze slope, soil, and path of the sun before drawing the first line.
  • Lifespan: We detail joints and roofs to withstand decades of high Northwest rainfall.
What We Do

Core architectural capabilities

Residential Architecture

Designing custom homes, micro-dwellings, and additions. We focus on natural cross-ventilation, daylighting, and layouts that adapt as family structures evolve.

Commercial & Workspace

Workspaces configured for concentration and collaboration. We replace conventional drywall layouts with glass, structural timber, and shared lightwells.

Interior Detailing

Custom millwork drawings, built-in shelving, cabinetry, and fixtures. We design furniture as permanent architectural elements integrated into the building frame.

Our Process

How we bridge the gap between design and delivery.

We do not produce drawing sets only to hand them off to builders we've never met. Our team spends significant time on site to resolve details collaboratively, minimizing errors during construction phases.

  1. 01

    Open-Book Estimates

    Detailed transparent cost reviews. We track labor costs, supplier bids, and contingencies directly to ensure project feasibility.

  2. 02

    Physical Scale Modeling

    Before finalizing architectural drawings, we build physical mockups to review how direct sunlight hits materials.

  3. 03

    On-Site Joint Resolution

    Our team drafts construction details alongside frame carpenters and stone masons directly at the job site.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you supervise construction on all projects?

Yes. We do not offer design-only services where we hand off blueprints. To maintain quality control, we manage all projects from early sketches to final builder walk-throughs.

How do you calculate your project fees?

We work on a fixed-fee scale tied to milestones rather than taking percentage commissions on construction costs. This aligns our interests with keeping your build budget in check.

Can you work with historic or heritage buildings?

Absolutely. Over half of our urban portfolio consists of renovations within historic districts. We specialize in blending contemporary concrete and steel with heritage brickwork.
